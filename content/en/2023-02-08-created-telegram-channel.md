I tried opening a Telegram channel.

I found that creating a channel on Telegram is very quick, and broadcasting to others is also very fast.

Most importantly, the platform is open, and after adding the bot function, updates from my personal website can be automatically synchronized to the channel.

Now, coupled with the real-time message reception, Telegram's functionality surpasses many other communication software programs. I highly recommend trying it!
