This little garden began as a simple idea: I wanted one quiet place for our days, our soft memories, and all the things that are too personal for the main site.

At first it was only a counter, counting from September 27, 2025. Then it slowly became more like a secret room: a page with flowers, falling petals, piano music, a memory timeline, and a soft cover image made only for our story.

![A soft abstract love garden cover](/our-garden-assets-k8f3q9p2/our-love-story-share-v2.png)

## Why I Built It

I did not want our memories to disappear into chat history or scattered photos. I wanted them to have a place where they could be found again, even after many ordinary days had passed.

The timeline became the simple entrance. A short note can stay there. A longer memory can become its own post. A photo, a YouTube video, or a link can sit beside the date. The page does not need to explain itself too much. It only needs to keep what matters.

## What Changed Along The Way

The first version was too plain. Then the page got softer: more transparent cards, more visible petals, flowers moving behind the text, and music that starts gently. The share image also changed until it felt more abstract and less like two specific people.

Later, the timeline became easier to keep. Dates display automatically, memories are grouped by year, and the page can search both timeline notes and longer love posts. Images can now open larger when clicked, so a small photo in a memory can become something worth pausing over.

## What This Place Is For

This garden is not meant to be perfect. It is meant to be kept alive.

Some days will only need one sentence. Some days will need a long post. All of them can stay here, quietly, in the same hidden corner.

And maybe that is enough: a small secret garden, made with patience, for the story we are still writing.
