10 years ago, I stopped writing my diaries on paper and change from notebook to digital software. I've gradually get unfamiliar with the feeling of holding a pen.   

These days, when I was transcripting my diaries, I felt the good feelings of my old handwritings. I missed them.    

I also get tedious of typing on the screen. There are a lot of distractions on digital devices now. I wanted to take a break from it.      

I bought a A6 notebook with dot format and started recording my habits and making to-do list on it.
