Stickers

﻿Why do I no longer use stickers? For me, the use of stickers distorts the understanding of others. I don’t think it’s an effective or appropriate way to communicate with others online. The Internet is already full of entertaining and useless information. Using stickers will make this situation worse. Using words is enough to convey accurate information. Although without stickers, some people will think that it will feel that there is no emotion. But the truth is that the Internet cannot emulate the real conversation. Calling and talking face to face may be a better way for communication.

Create a Telegram channel

I tried opening a Telegram channel. I found that creating a channel on Telegram is very quick, and broadcasting to others is also very fast. Most importantly, the platform is open, and after adding the bot function, updates from my personal website can be automatically synchronized to the channel. Now, coupled with the real-time message reception, Telegram’s functionality surpasses many other communication software programs. I highly recommend trying it!
